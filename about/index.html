About Me

Welcome to my blog.

I am Haozhe Zhu (朱浩哲).

Currently I am a Ph.D. student at Fudan University, advised by Xiaoyang Zeng in the State Key Laboratory of ASIC & System. I am also working closely with Chixiao Chen.

Most of my works lie in a overlap of artificial intelligence and integrated circuits.

Education & Experiences

In 2017, I received a B.Eng. degree in microelectronics from Fudan University.

  • 2021-Current, Biren Research
    Chip Architect Intern
  • 2016-2019, Brain-Chip Research Center, Fudan University
    Research assistant, with C.-J. Richard Shi
  • 2016, Shanghai Fudan Microelectronics Group Co., Ltd
    Security Lab Intern
